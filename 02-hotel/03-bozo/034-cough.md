# How would he cough?
Maybe with something dry.

# Dry?
Like, dust. The whole hotel should be full of it.

# Where do I get it?
In the restaurant (behind the reception) there is an old coffe maker. Give it a go. Apply the dust, *cough*, onto the pie.
